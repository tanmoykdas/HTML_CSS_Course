Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a country in South Asia, located on the Bay of Bengal.It is bordered by India to the north, east, west, and south, and by Myanmar to the southeast.

With a population of over 165 million people, Bangladesh is one of the most densely populated countries in the world.The country is known for its lush greenery, numerous waterways, and rich cultural heritage.

Bangladesh, a nation nestled in South Asia, is renowned for its lush landscapes, intricate river networks, and vibrant culture.

Despite facing challenges like poverty and natural disasters, Bangladesh has made remarkable strides in various sectors, including garment manufacturing and poverty reduction.

The country's rich history, evident in ancient ruins and historical sites, blends seamlessly with its modern aspirations. From the bustling capital city of Dhaka to the serene beauty of the Sundarbans mangrove forest, Bangladesh offers a diverse tapestry of experiences for visitors and residents alike.
